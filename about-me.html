Hello there!

My name is Kailey, but most people call me Kiki.

I’m a UX Designer in the Houston area with special interests in user research, accessible design, and front-end development. I finished a boot camp certificate course with Rice University in April of 2023 and can’t wait to get my career up and running!

Throughout my time as an educator, I deeply enjoyed helping my students learn and grow. And it’s this very same passion for people that initially led me to pursue UX Design. As I continue down this path, I hope that my career is defined by the accessibility and useability of the designs I create.

Here's a little bit about me.

I absolutely love...

  • Listening to the trees sway in the wind;
  • Watching my dogs experience pure joy on our walks;
  • Mid-summer car rides with the music up and the windows down;
  • Warm cups of tea on rainy days;
  • Getting hugs from my cat.

I'm not too fond of...

  • Deforestation;
  • Messy, cluttered spaces;
  • Harsh, white fluorescent lights;
  • Horrific Houston traffic;
  • Wastefulness.

So... what is Lucky Iris Designs?
